Hello, I'm Katherine!


The world is wild.

Do you ever feel like you're being watched? Well, you are. Welcome to the age of technology. If you're not being watched, you're being listened to. If you're not being listened to, your movements are being tracked. Sometimes, the thought of that is too much. I often want to run away to some hills in the middle of nowhere and begin a new, pastoral life with a garden and herbs and chickens. The village a couple miles away will suspect I'm a witch of some sort, but I won't be. Just some girl in the middle of nowhere. Happy and disconnected. Maybe I'll meet a cute wizard with a moving castle.

Who knows?

I do.

That's not going to happen.

But I do have Minecraft and Stardew Valley, so at least I can get close.

In the end, I use games as escapism for when I want to run away from my life. I want to be the reason someone else can get away too. I love creating stories and art to lose myself in. I just want to share them.
