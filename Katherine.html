Hello, I'm Katherine!



It's not that it changed, it's that we changed it.

-Masaki Suda, Long Hope Philia (translated by Shima Yuzuru)


Technology is such a pervasive aspect of our lives nowadays. It's amazing to see just what it can do in the fields of medicine, therapy, communication, and, of course, gaming. Hardware is getting stronger and more complex, processing power and CPUs are better than ever, and I can't help but marvel at our progress everytime I need a micro SD card. A chip, smaller than my thumbnail, 128 GB, and less than $20! I doubt someone from 80 years ago could even wrap their head around that.

But it continues to change!

Technology is changing so fast it seems hard to keep up sometimes. The next thing is always just around the corner, and we are forced to adapt to that. So we do. We have to. Because we do, technology doesn't slow down. Because we can adapt to the rapid-fire needs of the industry, technology can continue to evolve at it's breakneck pace.

Of course, keeping up isn't all we do. We innovate, we progress, we advance technology. We're getting closer and closer to a technologically unified world every day.


Who wouldn't want ot be a part of that?
